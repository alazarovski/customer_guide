Part B. Billing Documents

At each billing cycle, you will receive a structured set of billing documents. EUROCONTROL issues these documents in the middle of the month following the month in which the flights took place.

Billing Calendar

You can download the planned billing dates, payment deadlines, and claim submission windows from the official CRCO Billing Calendar.

Document Categories

The billing documents are organized into four main categories:

  • 1. Statements
  • 2. Invoices & Annexes
  • 3. Credit Notes & Annexes
  • 4. Information Documents
  • Statement of Account (ST): Records all accounting movements (charges, payments, interest) and is issued at least once a month. Daily intermediate statements can be downloaded via CEFA.
  • Bill (FB): Indicates the total amount of charges (including VAT) to be paid and the payment due date. Detailed bank details are printed on the reverse.
  • VAT Invoice (BT): Issued for tax purposes when VAT is invoiced on behalf of a Member State’s service provider. Note: This amount is already included in the Bill (FB) and must not be paid separately.
  • IOLP Bill (FI): Issued to apply Interest on Late Payment for charges not settled by the due date.
  • Pro Forma Statement (PF): Lists flights in chronological order, showing details for each flight, total route charges, and any applicable exemptions. The net sum equals the amount on the corresponding Bill (FB).
  • Bill & VAT Breakdown (CB): Presents a breakdown of route charges by charging zone and entity on behalf of which the bill was issued.
  • Credit Note (NC): Indicates the total amount of charges (including VAT) to be credited based on claimed flights.
  • VAT Credit Note (CT): Issued when VAT is credited on behalf of a service provider. Note: Already included in the Credit Note (NC).
  • IOLP Credit Note (NI): Issued to credit interest when a corresponding bill is adjusted.
  • Credit Note Pro Forma (NP): Lists credited flights in chronological order, matching the net sum on the Credit Note (NC).
  • Credit Note & VAT Breakdown (CN): Presents a breakdown of credited route charges per zone and entity.
  • Claim Result (DN): Communicates the CRCO’s decision to accept a claim (entailing a full credit and potential re-billing).
  • Claim Rejection Note: Issued when a claim is rejected, detailing why the original bill was correct. No financial transaction occurs.
  • Fleet Composition (FC): Displays the aircraft fleet and MTOW values on record as of the last day of the previous month. Used to establish the weight factor.

Understanding Document References

Every billing document issued by EUROCONTROL contains a structured reference number.

Example reference:

05 / 001776 / 30 / 2501 / 01

Reference Components

  1. Document Type / Charge System Code (Positions 1 & 3): Indicates the charge system or the document sub-type. These codes can appear in the first position (for standard system bills) or the third position (for credit notes, interest bills, or fleet documents).
  2. CRCO User Number (Position 2): Unique identifier assigned to the aircraft operator.
  3. Calendar Month (Position 4): The month in which the flights were performed, formatted as YYMM (e.g., 2501 represents January 2025).
  4. Bill Sequence Number (Position 5): Indicates the billing run for that flight month (01 = Initial bill; 02 = Additional bill for the same month).
