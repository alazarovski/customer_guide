Airspace Cost Recovery System

The Central Route Charges Office (CRCO) runs an efficient system for the cost recovery of air traffic services made available to aircraft operators. On behalf of EUROCONTROL’s Member States, the CRCO bills and collects route charges that fund air navigation facilities and services and supports Air Traffic Management developments.

Airspace Billing Pipeline: Flight to Settlement

Below is the standard lifecycle of airspace billing transactions handled by the CRCO:

<div class="step-num">1</div>
<h4>Flight Logged</h4>
<p>The Network Manager records flights in overflown charging zones, calculating precise coordinates and entry/exit points.</p>
<div class="step-num">2</div>
<h4>Charges Calculated</h4>
<p>Route, terminal, and communication charges are calculated using distance factors, MTOW weight factors, and monthly unit rates.</p>
<div class="step-num">3</div>
<h4>Consolidated Invoice</h4>
<p>Invoices, Pro Forma statements, and VAT breakdowns are generated in the middle of the following month in a single consolidated bill.</p>
<div class="step-num">4</div>
<h4>Secure Settlement</h4>
<p>Payments are made via bank transfer or credit card within 30 days. Claims for adjustments must be submitted within 60 days.</p>

CRITICAL SECURITY ALERT: FRAUD WARNING!

Fraudsters may attempt to contact you, claiming that EUROCONTROL bank accounts have changed, and try to persuade you to make payments to fake bank accounts.

  • Treat any communication with suspicion, especially if you are asked for financial information about your account or if a new bank account is communicated to you!
  • No Bank Account Changes Planned: EUROCONTROL has no plan to open new bank accounts in the foreseeable future and never communicates bank account numbers by e-mail, fax, or telephone.
  • Official Sources Only: Under no circumstances should you pay into any bank account other than those referenced on the official EUROCONTROL billing documents.
  • Verify Immediately: If in doubt, contact the Collection and Treasury departments immediately at +32.2.729.38.38.

For maximum transaction safety, we strongly advise operators to utilize the CEFA Portal to download bills, consult balances, and manage fleet data. Learn more in the CEFA Portal Guide.
