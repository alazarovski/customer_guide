Welcome

This website presents the EUROCONTROL CRCO Customer Guide to Charges, which provides detailed information on the different charges levied by the Central Route Charges Office (CRCO), billing documents, payment procedures, and submission of claims.

The Mission of the CRCO

The Central Route Charges Office (CRCO) runs an efficient system for the cost recovery of air traffic services made available to aircraft operators.

On behalf of EUROCONTROL’s Member States, the CRCO: * Bills and collects route charges that fund air navigation facilities and services and supports Air Traffic Management developments (see Part A: Route Charges). * Provides a single consolidated monthly bill in EUR, no matter how many EUROCONTROL Member States were overflown. * Bills and collects terminal charges on a bilateral basis on behalf of participating Member States (see Part A: Terminal Charges). * Bills and collects air navigation charges on behalf of non-Member States (see Part A: Air Navigation Charges). * Bills and collects communication charges in the Shanwick area (see Part A: Communication Charges).

The billing and recovery of air navigation charges by EUROCONTROL is indispensable in ensuring that air navigation facilities and services are steadily financed and safely operated, paving the way for the future evolution of the pan-European Air Traffic Management (ATM) system in the context of the Single European Sky and the European ATM Master Plan (SESAR).

FRAUD WARNING!

Fraudsters may attempt to contact you, claiming that EUROCONTROL bank accounts have changed, and try to persuade you to make payments to fake bank accounts.

  • Treat any communication with suspicion, especially if you are asked for financial information about your account or if a new bank account is communicated to you!
  • No Bank Account Changes Planned: EUROCONTROL has no plan to open new bank accounts in the foreseeable future and never communicates bank account numbers by e-mail, fax or telephone.
  • Official Sources Only: Under no circumstances should you pay into any bank account other than those referenced on the official EUROCONTROL billing documents. Never accept any other source of information.
  • Verification: If in doubt, contact us immediately at +32.2.729.38.38.

For increased security, we strongly advise you to use our CEFA portal, which allows you to download your billing documents, consult your account’s latest status, submit modifications of aircraft data, or introduce claims and monitor their status. Learn more in the CEFA Portal section.
