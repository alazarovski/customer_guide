Part C. Payment of Charges

All charges billed by EUROCONTROL are issued on the same date monthly, and payment must be received at the latest 30 days from the issue date of the bill. Due dates and bank details are printed on each bill.

C.1. General Remarks

Security and Fraud Prevention
  • Official Bank Accounts Only: Pay only into bank accounts listed on bills or statements downloaded directly from CEFA, or received by post on secured paper bearing the EUROCONTROL logo.
  • Security Policy: EUROCONTROL never communicates bank account numbers by e-mail, fax, or telephone.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: If you suspect a fraudulent payment request, contact us immediately at +32.2.729.38.38.
  • Separate Accounts: There are separate bank accounts for the route charges system and for each State under terminal, air navigation, and communication charges.
  • Transfer Fees: All bank charges are borne by the user. EUROCONTROL credits operator accounts with the net amount received on its bank accounts.
  • Payment Date: Payments are deemed received on the value date on which they are credited to EUROCONTROL’s bank account.

C.2. Payment by Bank Transfer

  • Payment Details: Ensure payments are in the correct currency, to the correct bank account, and quote the reference of the statement of account or bill.
  • Interest Payments: No bill reference is required for Interest on Late Payment (IOLP) bills; specify the word “Interest” in the transfer details. Payments will be allocated to the oldest interest bill first.
  • Payment Allocation: In the absence of a remittance advice, payments are automatically allocated first to the oldest outstanding bills, and then to interest.
  • Remittance Advice: Send payment details and allocation instructions to r5.crco@eurocontrol.int.

C.3. Payment by Credit Card

Aircraft operators can pay bills via credit card. Currently, only Visa and Mastercard are accepted.

Warning

Interest On Late Payments (IOLP) bills cannot be paid by credit card.

C.3.1. Procedure

To pay bills using a credit card:

  1. Log in to your CEFA account.
  2. Register a Credit Card:
    • Navigate to the “Billing Information” screen.
    • Scroll to the “Credit Cards linked to your account” box.
    • Approve the Terms & Conditions.
    • Click “Register a new credit card” and follow the prompts.
    • Note: A temporary test transaction of 0.01 EUR is processed to verify the card (not charged). Up to 3 cards can be registered per user.
  3. Navigate to Detailed Financial Situation:
    • Locate the “Pay by Credit Card” button at the top right.
  4. Select Payment Option:
  • Option A: Pay Individual Bills
  • Option B: Recurring Payment Instructions
  • Select individual bills to pay.
  • Choose the registered credit card.
  • Click “Confirm payment instructions” (bottom right).
  • Bills will appear greyed out until the transaction is fully processed.
  • Set up automated payments.
  • Link specific credit cards to specific charge systems (e.g., Route, France Terminal, etc.).
  • Cards are charged twice a month (around the 6th and 28th) to ensure settlement before the due date.

Important Card Rules

  • Admin Fee: A 3% administration fee is added to the bill total to cover third-party card provider costs.
  • Processing Time: Credit card payments can take up to 5 working days to reflect on your statement of account.
  • Declined Transactions: If a transaction is declined, it will be cancelled. Persistent declines will result in the cancellation of recurrent payment instructions.
